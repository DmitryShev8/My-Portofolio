## When the Signal Dies

Unlike what we see in the 2012 movie, we won’t see a meteor strike and many people die instantly. We couldn’t expect to end as the dinosaurs did. Even though the fact is they are extinct because of the aftermath of meteor strikes, they didn’t die by meteor strikes alone.

It may start with no signal, no GPS, no internet. The world will go insane if this happens. At the beginning of this, we have no choice but to rethink our survival instinct. We use our phones for everything; it would be the end of the world if this tool were no longer useful.

In a real collapse, whatever the causes, it could be a nuclear war, solar disaster, or infrastructure failures. Phones are no longer working as a tool of survival. The worst possible case is when you used to live alone and connected with people online. If you lost them, it would be hard to connect with the people in real life. The messenger app we use today is the most important communication tool. We can see where they are and how they are doing, and you can also easily invite them to meet at a specific place. What could we do if our messenger app is gone forever? We rarely know each other's houses nowadays. Then, it could be worse. You have nowhere to go.

Our bad phone habits could come back to bite us. Doom scrolling on social media like TikTok and Instagram. It would be a psychological disaster if we no longer received information as fast as we do today. Finding a new habit forcefully is not easy. Our brains are already used to easy tasks; forming a harder-working habit will be a pain in the ass.

## Friendly Reminder

World War 3 is close. We still don’t know how it will start and end. Having a real connection in real life might be the priority solution. They could be our source of information if the internet is no longer there. And we should also learn basic survival skills, such as cooking and maintaining a fit body, whatever keeps your body healthy. Since the human era of hunter-gatherers, all we care about is having a fit body by hunting real food. And that’s it, like other animals do. It is ironic that the young generation today easily gets sick because of diabetes and obesity. This is proof that we are not well prepared for our own bodies. So, knowing what your body is capable of is also important.
